Sign up in 15 minutes

Create a free merchant account. Link your bank. We generate a QR code for your register — that's your new payment terminal. No hardware to buy.

2

Place the QR code at checkout

Print it as a table tent, counter sign, or display it on a tablet. Your staff says: "Save 5% by scanning the QR code." That's the entire script.

3

Get paid instantly

When a customer pays, USDC (digital dollars) arrive in your merchant account. Auto-converts to USD. Deposits to your bank the same day — not 2-3 business days.

4

Keep your current system

ZAP runs alongside Square, Toast, Clover — whatever you already use. Customers who prefer cards still pay with cards. ZAP just adds a cheaper option.

For Customers

1

Download a free wallet app

2

Add money from your bank

Link your bank account or debit card. Buy USDC — a digital dollar that's always worth exactly $1.00. It's not Bitcoin, it doesn't fluctuate.

3

Scan & pay

At checkout, scan the merchant's QR code, enter the amount, confirm. Done in 10 seconds. Faster than inserting a chip card.

4

Save 3-5% every time

Merchants pass their fee savings to you as a discount. A family spending $200/week saves $400-500 per year — better than any credit card rewards program.
