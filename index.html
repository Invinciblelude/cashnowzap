What happens if something goes wrong?

Payment sent to wrong address?

ZAP-generated QR codes are merchant-specific and amount-locked. Mis-sends are technically impossible through our system. If a customer manually sends to a wrong wallet, our support team traces the on-chain transaction and assists recovery.

Merchant wants a refund processed?

One-click refund from your ZAP dashboard. USDC returns to the customer's wallet in under 60 seconds. No dispute process. No fees.

USDC loses its peg?

USDC is backed by cash and US Treasury securities held at regulated banks (BNY Mellon). In 10+ years, USDC has maintained its $1.00 peg. Circle is publicly traded (NYSE: CRCL) and federally regulated. This is not an algorithmic stablecoin.

Solana network goes down?

ZAP monitors network health in real-time. If Solana experiences downtime, transactions queue and process when the network resumes (typically minutes). Card payments continue working as your fallback.
