## The Opportunity

US enterprises pay an estimated $24.7B/year in credit card interchange fees (conservative, at 1.75% effective rate on $1.41T in combined revenue across the top 7 targets). Stablecoin payments cut merchant processing to 0.5-1%. The savings delta is 1.0-1.35% per transaction.

The infrastructure is live: Circle USDC ($75.3B circulation, NYSE: CRCL), Visa USDC settlement on Solana ($4.5B annualized), Stripe+Bridge ($1.1B acquisition), and the GENIUS Act (signed into law July 18, 2025).

What's missing: a company that sells, implements, and scales stablecoin checkout at the enterprise level.

**ZAP fills that gap.**

---

## What ZAP Does

ZAP is an AI-powered crypto payment technology company. We help enterprises replace legacy card processing with USDC stablecoin payments — reducing fees by 50-80% with instant settlement.

**Products:**
- **ZAP Checkout** — white-label stablecoin payment system (QR, in-app, API-based)
- **ZAP Dashboard** — AI analytics for fee savings, adoption prediction, compliance
- **ZAP Pilot** — turnkey 90-day implementation for enterprise clients
- **ZAP Loyalty** — on-chain rewards system integrated with existing loyalty programs

---

## Proof of Concept

Live restaurant pilot using NowPayments + USDC on Solana:
- Processing fee: dropped from 2.35% to 0.5% (80% reduction)
- Settlement: instant (vs. 2-3 business days with cards)
- Customer experience: QR scan, confirm, done
- Gas cost per transaction: $0.00025-$0.005 (Solana)
